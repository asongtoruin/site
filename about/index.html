Hello!

I’m Adam. By day a transport planner, by night a self-taught amateur programmer. In both situations I try and use Python as much as possible, though once I’ve got a few projects out of the way (such as this site) I’m planning on learning R.

This site is intended mostly as a place to detail the various projects I end up working on for the handful of people who ask me how they’re done. It’s probably not going to be super interesting or updated with any well-defined regularity, but hopefully there might be something someone out there finds informative.
